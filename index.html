Dice Game Rules

  • If the player rolls one of the dices a 1 or double 6, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other numbers, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score, and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • The first player to score(100 Default you can change it) or more points wins.
